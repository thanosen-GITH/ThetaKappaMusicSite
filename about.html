About

Thanos Karakantas

Theta Kappa Music brings together musical work, Tonmeister practice, and creative software projects in one connected profile.

Thanos Karakantas works across composition, arrangement, performance, audio engineering, and rhythm/audio software. The site is being developed as a central place for music, selected audio credits, and tools such as BRG and Rhythm Combinations.

This page will become a more personal self-portrait: the musical background, the Tonmeister path, and the software work behind the creative tools. The goal is to show these areas as parts of one practice rather than separate professional identities.
