Profile

I work where musical judgment and audio technology meet: balancing performers, systems, rooms, timing, communication, and repeatable workflows. My background began with instruments, MIDI programming, home recording, synthesizers, and DAW production, then developed through computer science, advanced music studies, and Tonmeister studies at the Universitat der Kunste Berlin.

That combination shapes the way I approach audio work. I am comfortable moving between artistic listening and technical detail: FOH, playback, recording, editing, troubleshooting, signal flow, acoustic reality, and the practical demands of rehearsals and performances.
